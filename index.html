Our Mission

TimerPro was built with one goal: to give everyone a world-class timer and stopwatch that just works — instantly, beautifully, and without friction. No accounts. No subscriptions. No bloat.

We believe productivity tools should be accessible to everyone, everywhere, in their own language. That's why we support 15 languages and counting.
